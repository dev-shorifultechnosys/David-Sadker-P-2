Welcome

Five decades of teaching, writing, and listening.

David Sadker is an author, Professor Emeritus, and workshop leader who has spent more than fifty years at the intersection of education, equity, and the everyday lives of students.

With his late wife Myra Sadker, David authored landmark work on gender bias in education, including Failing at Fairness. Today, writing from the Hawaiian coast, he continues that work through speaking, mentoring, and a forthcoming book.
