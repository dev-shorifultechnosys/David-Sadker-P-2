Forthcoming

A new book — quietly in the works.

The title is still under wraps. The heart of it isn’t. After decades in classrooms, lecture halls, and living rooms, this is the book David has been circling for a long time.

Email David about the book
What it’s about

A long conversation, gathered into one volume.

Drawing on five decades of research, teaching, and listening — to students, teachers, parents, and colleagues — the book gathers what David has learned about what really happens in classrooms, and what we can still change.

It’s part memoir, part field guide, part argument. It’s written for anyone who has ever wondered why some children get noticed and others get overlooked, and what it would take to do better.
